MISSION

MISSION


Masters of Business Administration was established to bring together a community of students with interests in business and entrepreneurship. More than just a simple club, MBA has weekly meetings involving discussions of the elements and facets of business. Beyond discussions, MBA aims to be an idea incubator for students. Members are given the opportunity to present business ideas, and subsequently work together on developing such ideas into reality. With the help of club leadership, members can even seek funding for their commercial ideas. MBA also possesses a competitive aspect; there is always an aim to succeed in different business competitions. All of the meetings, idea incubations, and competitions stem from the root of MBA's mission: to foster and develop the entrepreneurial business spirit and its many facets within the Masters community.




ASPECTS

DISCUSSIONS

Weekly meetings, every Tuesday from 4:45 to 6:00 PM, to discuss club agendas and business plans.

IDEA INCUBATOR

Develop your business ideas with other club members. Create a plan with us.

COMPETITIONS

MBA has the goal of participating in and winning awards at entrepreneurship seminars and competitions.

FUNDING

If you create a strong business plan and intend to turn it into reality, you can apply for a club grant! Club grants are loaned to fund ideas.

ANYONE CAN JOIN

This club is for any member of the Masters community; you can be completely new to business or a young Warren Buffet, you are still welcome.
